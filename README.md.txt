# 🚨 Fuel Crisis Impact Analysis (Power BI Project)

## 📊 Problem Statement
Rising fuel prices have significantly increased transportation and delivery costs for restaurants. This project analyzes how fuel price fluctuations impact overall revenue, cost structure, and profitability.

---

## 📁 Dataset
The analysis is based on:
- Fuel price data (daily/weekly trends)
- Restaurant sales data
- Delivery cost data

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Understand the relationship between fuel price and delivery cost  
- Identify impact on profit margins  
- Detect low-performing or low-margin menu items  
- Provide data-driven business recommendations  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- 📌 Delivery costs increased significantly with rising fuel prices  
- 📌 Profit margins decreased during fuel price spikes  
- 📌 Certain menu items became low-margin or loss-making  
- 📌 High delivery cost directly impacted overall revenue  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- Fuel price trend analysis  
- Revenue vs cost comparison  
- Profit margin tracking  
- Item-level performance analysis  

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used
- Power BI (Data Visualization & Dashboard)
- Microsoft Excel (Data Cleaning & Preparation)

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Remove or reprice low-margin menu items  
- Optimize delivery routes to reduce fuel usage  
- Introduce dynamic pricing based on fuel trends  
- Focus on high-profit items during high fuel cost periods
